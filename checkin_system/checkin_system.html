Before You Start: Important Prerequisites

To ensure everything works perfectly, please make sure you follow these two simple rules:

  • Use Your Phone: This system is designed for mobile. Please only use your smartphone to check in and out, not a desktop or laptop.
  • Enable Location/GPS: Your phone's location services must be turned ON for AppSheet. This is crucial for recording accurate location data for your visits.

The 'Check In / Out' View

In the first tab of the app, you will see two main buttons to start and end your day.

Step 1: How to Check In

Checking in marks the start of your workday and records your location and time automatically.

1. Tap the 'Check In' button.

2. A confirmation pop-up will appear. Tap 'Check In' again to confirm.

That's it! You're checked in for the day. The system automatically records your time and location.

Step 2: How to Check Out & Log Your Sales

At the end of your day, check out to log your sales activity. This replaces the old Google Sheet.

1. Tap the 'Check Out' button and confirm in the pop-up modal.

2. The daily sales form will open. Fill in all the details about your day's work.

3. After submitting the form, you'll see a success message. Your checkout is complete!

See Your Progress: The Weekly Dashboard

At the bottom of the app, you'll find your new 'Weekly Dashboard'. This view shows you all your activity for the week.

You can see your check-in status, location, total sales, visits, and all other details you've logged during your check-outs.

Very Important Reminders

Once a Day Only

Please Check In only ONCE when you start your day and Check Out only ONCE when you finish.

Already Checked In?

If you're not sure if you've checked in, just look at the Weekly Dashboard. Your 'Checkin Status' will show your location if you're already in!
