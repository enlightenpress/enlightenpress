Internal Documentation

Enlighten Press, Field Operations Tooling

A connected suite of tools that prepares the data, runs the field, and audits the outcome. Five documents cover the systems involved, who uses them, and how they hand off to each other.

01The documents

Each tool has its own document. The first three are technical references; the fourth blends technical and operational; the last is a step-by-step guide written for sales reps.

01 Technical

Australia Leadplanning App

AppSheet · Slices · Map Filter table

Mobile-first field CRM that surfaces only actionable leads (Schools and Childcares) to AU sales reps. Slices drive a dynamic map; a single-row "Map Filter" table acts as a remote control. Includes 4–5 month cool-down logic that hides leads after a "no over the phone" so reps don't burn the relationship.

02 Technical

Odometer Tracker

Google Apps Script · Google Sheets · Drive

Web app for tracking driving mileage for reimbursement. Reps submit check-in and check-out odometer readings with photo evidence; the system computes daily kilometres. Manager view groups data into Mon–Fri working weeks with running totals, averages, and inline odometer photos pulled from Drive.

03 Technical

Universal Planning Cleaner

Python · stdlib only · CSV pipeline

Self-contained app used by ops to clean and merge messy data before it reaches reps. Joins manual visit logs, the master database, and MYOB exports; normalisation algorithms fix broken IDs and de-duplicate. Computes a Priority tag (BEST, HIGH, DEAD, …) and overrides DEAD to BOTTOM if MYOB shows the centre recently paid.

04 Technical + Operations

Sales Representative App

AppSheet · Bots · Hidden audit columns

Field operations manual covering the broader CRM workflows, with emphasis on automation, audits, and fraud prevention. An AppSheet bot snapshots every lead edit into a hidden Rep Visits table; the check-in flow locks GPS coordinates and millisecond timestamps the moment the odometer photo is taken, blocking retroactive or spoofed entries.

02How it all fits together

The five tools form one pipeline: ops cleans the data, the field acts on it, and the system audits the outcome end-to-end.

Stage 1 · Data prep

Operations cleans the inputs

Ops runs the Universal Planning Cleaner to untangle sales and accounting data, producing a clean, prioritised call list with reliable IDs and MYOB-aware priority tags.

Stage 2 · Field execution

Reps work the prioritised leads

Reps open the Leadplanning App to navigate a dynamic map of those leads, with cool-down rules hiding any centre that recently said "no over the phone."

Stage 3 · Day in / day out

Hours, sales, and visits get logged

Reps follow the Check-In/Out manual to log their day through the Sales Representative App. The app captures GPS and timestamps at the moment of action, and a bot snapshots every lead edit for the audit trail.

Stage 4 · Reimbursement

Managers verify and pay

Mileage flows into the Odometer Tracker, where managers see weekly running totals, averages, and the underlying odometer photos — so reimbursements are tied to verifiable evidence.
